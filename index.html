Welcome

Join us for a special session on AI in Cultural and Artistic Heritage at MLSP 2025.

Call for Papers

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming the field of cultural and artistic heritage, offering new possibilities for the analysis, classification, and restoration of historical artifacts. This session will showcase the latest AI-driven methodologies and their groundbreaking applications in the analysis, interpretation, and preservation of cultural and artistic heritage. We invite researchers to contribute their work on AI techniques applied to textual, visual, and multimodal data within the context of cultural heritage.

Topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Image Analysis & Artifact Classification
  • AI-driven Restoration and Enhancement of Cultural Artifacts
  • Machine Learning for Document Understanding
  • Generative Models for Historical Artifacts
  • Multimodal AI Approaches for Cultural Heritage Connoisseurship
  • AI in Art History and Art Analysis

Important Dates (Tentative):

  • Deadline for paper submissions: May 10, 2025
  • Notification of paper acceptance: June 24, 2025
  • Deadline for camera-ready paper submissions: July 15, 2025
