Welcome

Join us for a special session on AI in Cultural and Artistic Heritage at MLSP 2025.

Call for Papers

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming the field of cultural and artistic heritage, offering new possibilities for the analysis, classification, and restoration of historical artifacts. This session will showcase the latest AI-driven methodologies and their groundbreaking applications in the analysis, interpretation, and preservation of cultural and artistic heritage.

Topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Image Analysis & Artifact Classification
  • AI-driven Restoration and Enhancement of Cultural Artifacts
  • Machine Learning for Document Understanding
  • Computational Analysis of Historical Paintings
